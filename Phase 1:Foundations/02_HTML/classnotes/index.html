Delicious Recipes

Recipe 1: Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • Instructions:
    • Preheat the oven to 350°F.
    • In a small bowl, mix together the flour and baking soda.
    • In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
    • Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs.
    • Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing well.
    • Stir in the chocolate chips.
    • Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
    • Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, or until golden brown.
    • Cool on wire racks.

Recipe 2: Tomato Pasta

Ingredients Instructions
  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet.
  7. Toss the pasta with the tomato sauce until well coated.
  8. Serve hot.
